## 邀請講者 (Invited Speakers)

### Plenary Speakers

**Dr. Chih-Ming Ho（何志明院士）**
Taiwan's leading figure in semiconductor and nanotechnology. Academician with contributions to microelectronics, nanodevices, and advanced fabrication. Key role in bridging academia and industry. Member of Academia Sinica.

**Prof. Zhenan Bao（鮑哲南教授）**
Stanford University. World-renowned materials scientist, pioneer in organic and flexible electronics. Research on stretchable, wearable, and skin-like electronic devices. Member of National Academy of Engineering and National Academy of Sciences.

**Prof. Weileun Fang（方維倫教授）**
National Tsing Hua University. Chair Professor, IEEE Fellow (2015). 20+ years in MEMS. ~500 refereed papers, ~120 patents. Former General Chair of World Micromachine Summit 2012, IEEE Sensors 2012, Transducers 2017. Chief Editor of JMM.

### Keynote Speakers

**Prof. Yoko Yamakoshi**
ETH Zürich, Department of Chemistry and Applied Biosciences. Ph.D. from University of Tokyo. Postdoc at ETH Zürich with Prof. François Diederich. Professor since 2014.

**Prof. Jihyun Kim**
Seoul National University, Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering. Member of the National Academy of Engineering of Korea. Fellow of the Electrochemical Society (ECS). Technical Editor for ECS JSST.

**Dr. Reinhard Voelkel**
SUSS MicroOptics SA, Switzerland. Expert in micro-optics, microlens arrays, and wafer-level optics. Bridging precision optical engineering with semiconductor manufacturing.

## 研究主題 (Conference Topics)

### A. Nanoelectronics, Materials and Device Physics
- A1. Surface/Interface Functional Electronics
- A2. Nanodevice Physics
- A3. Micro/Nanoelectronics Materials and Devices
- A4. Sensing-Memory-Computing Devices

### B. Micro/Nano Fabrication and Advanced Manufacturing
- B1. Micro/Nanofabrication and Sensor
- B2. Micro/Nanofabrication Techniques and Devices
- B3. 3D Printing and Bioprinting

### C. MEMS/NEMS Devices, Mechanics and Transduction
- C1. M/NEMS Resonators
- C2. Non-linear Mechanisms
- C3. Piezoelectric and Acoustic MEMS
- C4. Electromechanical Transduction
- C5. Inertial Sensors

### D. Sensors, Biosensors and Biomedical Systems
- D1. Micro Biosensing
- D2. Flexible Biosensors
- D3. Chemical Sensor
- D4. Flexible Functional Materials
- D5. Biomedical Optical Imaging

### E. Integrated Systems, Circuits and RF Devices
- E1. Integrated Devices and Circuit Design
- E2. RF Micro/Nanodevices

### F. Emerging Systems: Robotics, AI and Bioinspired Technologies
- F1. Micro/Nano Robotics
- F2. AI for M/NEMS
- F3. Biomimetic MEMS
- F4. Energy Devices / Self-powered

## 場地規劃

- **主場館：** 國立清華大學 學習資源中心（旺宏館）
- **1F 國際會議廳：** Plenary / Keynote 演講

  - 主題：奈微米系統創新創業及應用、感測器物聯網、專利情報及新醫材法規
