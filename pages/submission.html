Abstract Submission

Prospective authors are invited to submit a two-page abstract for review. Authors of accepted abstracts will be invited to submit a full paper (4–6 pages).

Submission System


The online submission system will be available soon.

Submission Guidelines

  • Two-column IEEE conference format, A4 paper size
  • Abstract: 2 pages maximum
  • Full paper: 4–6 pages (up to 8 pages with extra charges)
  • PDF format only, maximum 5 MB
  • Minimum resolution: 300 DPI for all figures
  • Top margin: 36 mm; Bottom and side margins: 19 mm

Plagiarism Policy

All submissions will be screened using iThenticate. Papers with significant overlap with previously published work will be rejected.
